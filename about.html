Hi! I'm Erika!

I love building websites and taking photos!

My first camera...

I discovered photography in a Happy Meal. The lime green camera came with the meal (chicken nuggets...I believe) and it started my obsession with taking beautiful photos. I took photos of everything! My family, our pets, random stranger’s pets, trees… everything! Since my dad is a photographer, he passed down one of his older film cameras to me when I was 12 and taught me some tips and tricks. Now I focus on family portraits and sport photos. My first camera was in a Happy Meal and now photography is my Happy Place.

My first website...

I taught myself HTML in when I was high school. I was curious about how the Internet worked and how websites were built. My dad bought me my first book on HTML and I taught myself. I built my first website at age 15 for my friends’ sports teams. Recently, I started working in the tech industry and fell back in love with the tech world. I have recently started taking HackerYou courses to learn how to build amazingly, wonderful, creative, static websites. What I love most is how you can take letters and characters and build something beautiful.
